Channeling the power of habits.

Is a self-proclaimed sustainable company really sustainable if, at a macro level, the individuals that form the company do not actively participate in a greener lifestyle?

That is the challenge Acer brought our team two years ago. The idea was clear: to create a more sustainable lifestyle, one has to partake in sustainable habits. The question then became: how do we form greener habits?

Here comes Earth Mission, a tracker app that breaks down the process of creating and maintaining habits, turning them into simple actions to complete daily. Users can join company and friendsâ€™ challenges to help maintain the effort through community effect. A point system controls user rankings, badges and rewards, and character customization.

The main challenge for the user experience was making an interface as intuitive and easy to use as possible for most. Target audiences were multiple, spanning several generations and ranking both extremes of the tech literacy spectrum, with their main common denominator being the company they are part of.
